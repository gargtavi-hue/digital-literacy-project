# STAY SAFE ONLINE – UPI FRAUD PREVENTION CHECKLIST

1. Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone.
2. Do not accept unknown payment requests.
3. Always verify the identity of the caller before taking any action.
4. Avoid clicking on suspicious links received via SMS or email.
5. Use official banking apps only.
6. Regularly check your bank account statements.
7. Enable app lock and two-factor authentication.
8. Be cautious while scanning QR codes from unknown sources.

# Financial Safety Tips:
- Remember that receiving money does not require entering a PIN.
- Always double-check before approving any payment request.
