# DESCRIPTION
I created this digital literacy infographic using Canva. It is an online design tool that offers many templates and visual elements. 

The infographic highlights the key topics such as what digital literacy means, safe internet practices, useful digital tools for students, professional online presence, and email etiquette.

I used simple language, icons, and a clean layout to make the information easy to understand and visually appealing. 

While designing, I found it challenging to include all the important points.  However, this helped me improve my ability to present information in a very clear and concise manner. Overall, this task increased my creativity and helped me understand the importance of spreading digital awareness effectively.
