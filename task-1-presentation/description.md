# DESCRIPTION
I created this digital literacy infographic using Canva, an online design tool that offers a variety of templates and visual elements. 
The infographic highlights key topics such as what digital literacy means, safe internet practices, useful digital tools for students, professional online presence, and email etiquette.
I used simple language, icons, and a clean layout to make the information easy to understand and visually appealing. 
While designing, I found it challenging to include all important points without overcrowding the slide. 
However, this helped me improve my ability to present information in a clear and concise manner. Overall, this task enhanced my creativity and helped me understand the importance of spreading digital awareness effectively.
