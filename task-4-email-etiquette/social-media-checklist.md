# PART-B 
# SOCIAL MEDIA DO’S AND DON’TS FOR COLLEGE STUDENTS

# DO’S:
1. Maintain a professional and respectful tone in your posts and comments.
2. Use privacy settings to control who can view your content.
3. Share informative, positive, and meaningful content.
4. Think before posting to ensure it does not harm your reputation.
5. Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
6. Build a positive digital footprint that reflects your personality and goals.

# DON’TS:
1. Do not share personal information like phone numbers, address, or passwords.
2. Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content.
3. Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments.
4. Avoid clicking on suspicious links or downloading unknown files.
5. Do not overshare daily activities or sensitive moments.
6. Avoid posting content that may negatively impact your future career.
